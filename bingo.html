How to play
1. Watch the Live Stream — lore items drift across the screen.
2. Recognise an item from your card? Head to the Tracker and daub it.
3. Five in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) — call BINGO!
4. All players watching at the same time see the same items, so results are verifiable.
